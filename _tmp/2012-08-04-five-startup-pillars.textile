A good idea is not enough, and in certain cases not even needed right away. You need good solutions to the following points:

# Idea
# Business model
# Execution
# Distribution
# Virality

If any of the pillars are missing the startup will wobble and most likely fail.

There are exceptions to the rule. Instagram - but you could say that their business model was &quot;Get bought by Facebook&quot;.
